Facades

By Industhan

11.12.13.14 Dec 2025

At Conscious Collective 2025, Industhan reimagines façades as instruments of cool. Their ceramic modules intercept heat before it reaches the interior, creating naturally ventilated, comfortable spaces without relying on air conditioning. The installation is a quiet demonstration of a simple truth: when buildings are kept cool from the outside, the inside follows. Crafted through a blend of traditional handmade processes and precise design, each module responds to orientation, context, and climate. The system transforms façades into active participants in the city’s thermal comfort, reducing energy use while celebrating materiality, craft, and innovation. Through this work, Industhan offers a vision of urban design that is both sustainable and elegantly resilient.
