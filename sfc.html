Jaise jyada garmi padta hai June-july mein; fridge hi thanda hota hai, cooling hoti hai usme. Toh jaise koi samaan nikalne jati hoon, toh aise dil karta hai use khol ke bas baithi rahu uske saamne

This is Kanchan, a home-maker from north-western Delhi, sharing how she finds small ways to keep herself cool while doing her daily household work.

For many women like her, indoor heat adds a hidden burden to care work. Kitchens and living spaces often trap heat with little ventilation or access to cooling.

At Conscious Collective 2025, our exhibit documents how women endure, adapt and keep their homes running in extreme heat. Join us from 11 to 14 December in Mumbai
