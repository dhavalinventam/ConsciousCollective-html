The Force Within

By Bandana Jain

11.12.13.14 Dec 2025

Artist and sustainability visionary Bandana Jain presents The Force Withinâ€”a sculptural meditation on resilience and the power we hold to shape the future. Known for transforming corrugated cardboard into works of elegance and depth, Bandana here invites us to confront the climate crisis through a symbol both intimate and universal.

Crafted entirely from recycled cardboard, the work carries both message and medium: porous, breathable, and adaptive, it embodies the very qualities we seek in climate-responsive design. The Force Within does not solve, it aims to awaken. It reminds us that what we release from our hands today will decide whether tomorrow burns, or breathes.
