Designing Desirable Futures Through Circular Innovation

Parametrics In The Age Of Heat

13 Dec 2025 | 11:00 AM

Arthur Mamou Mani

As the climate crisis intensifies, design must go beyond aesthetics to create solutions that are sustainable, adaptive, and regenerative. This talk explores how circular thinking and parametric design can be combined to respond meaningfully to the challenges of a warming planet and shrinking resources.

Participants will discover how creative tools and computational intelligence can help craft projects that are both bold and responsible, leveraging circular processes, sustainable materials, and innovative fabrication techniques. The session will also invite reflection on the bigger question: What does a desirable future look like, and how can we design it, not just for today, but for tomorrow?

Join us to explore the intersection of sustainability, technology, and purposeful design, where creativity meets climate-conscious innovation.
