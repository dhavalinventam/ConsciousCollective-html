Punah 3.0​

By EcoLattice X Godrej

11.12.13.14 Dec 2025

At Conscious Collective 2025, EcoLattice brings its global journey back home with Punah 3.0, a showcase that reimagines the materials we live with and the futures they shape. Known for its pioneering work with 3D-printed foam alternatives in London’s Beyond Foam exhibition, EcoLattice now expands the conversation by presenting a collection of experimental materials sourced from across India, cow dung composites, salt-based PCM panels, solar-reflective coatings, and more.

The exhibit is not just a display of objects but an invitation to rethink waste, resilience, and comfort. Visitors will experience how unlikely materials, often overlooked or discarded, can become building blocks of a cooler, more sustainable world. Punah 3.0 asks us to imagine design not as extraction, but as renewal: a practice where innovation is measured not by what is made new, but by what is remade with care.
