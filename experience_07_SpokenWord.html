Spoken Word Performance

By Kommune

14 Dec 2025 | 01:00 PM - 01:40 PM

Kommune brings a special spoken-word duet to Conscious Collective, an intimate, emotionally charged performance led by the commanding voices of Mahi G and Nishu Dixit.

Mahi’s bold, rhythmic storytelling meets Nishu’s quiet, contemplative poetry to create a tapestry of experiences that move between the personal and the planetary. Through themes of identity, resilience, and our shifting relationship with nature, they explore how climate change is not just an environmental crisis, but a deeply human one.

Together, they build a space that is urgent yet tender, where stories become mirrors, questions become invitations, and the audience is drawn into a collective moment of reflection. Expect to leave stirred, moved, and compelled to see the world a little more clearly.
