This installation pairs lndusthan's ceramic facade system with lnterio by Godrej's furniture to demonstrate how spatial comfort and cooling can emerge directly from material and design intelligence. The facade modules block direct heat gain and improve natural ventilation, reducing and in some cases eliminating the need for air-conditioning. Their performance lies in how the modules are assembled, with forms engineered for climate, sun path, and architectural intent.

Within this cool, shaded environment, lnterio's furniture creates a soothing, conversational space where ergonomic design supports slower, more meaningful interaction. Together, the facade and furniture show how material intelligence and modern making can shape spaces that stay cool not through energy use, but through design.

lndusthan is a design-led practice creating climate-responsive, materially intelligent architecture rooted in Indian sensibilities. Drawing from handmade processes and contemporary technology, their work blends craft with engineering to address heat, environment, and context. Committed to knowledge-sharing, the studio nurtures emerging designers while shaping sustainable, culturally grounded solutions for India's evolving built landscape.
