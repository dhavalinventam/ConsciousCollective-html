Clay Moulding

By Udayachal Primary School

Clay is one of the simplest, yet most powerful, mediums of expression. In the hands of children, it becomes more than play, it’s therapy, imagination, and learning, all at once. As they roll, press, and shape, they discover patience, focus, and the quiet joy of making something from nothing.

At Conscious Collective, students of Udayachal Primary School will guide a clay moulding activity where each child can craft a small artifact of their own — a keepsake to carry home. And while it begins as an exercise for children, it often draws in adults too, reminding us that creativity knows no age.
