Supercool

Reclaiming Cool Through Dignity, Access, Beauty, And Survival.

12 Dec 2025

Ayaz Basrai

This conversation offers a strategic foresight perspective, an exploration of how we’ve arrived at our current paradigm of addressing global challenges, and the mindset shift needed to engage with them meaningfully.

Through stories from the emerging future, it paints a canvas of radical possibilities, glimpses of a world that is more equitable, regenerative, and deeply interconnected. The conversation invites us to expand our vision of what’s achievable, to not only cool our homes and cities, but also our collective imagination.
