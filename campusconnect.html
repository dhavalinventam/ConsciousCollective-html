Campus Connect

Architecture + Design X Conscious Collective

13 Dec - 15 Dec 2024

"With the thought of a ‘climate conscious future’ at the center of their theme, Conscious Collective Campus Connect in collaboration with Architecture + Design, invites students from across institutes in the country to send in their ideas & designs for working model installations to be showcased at the Conscious Collective 2024. A jury of leading architects and design professionals will shortlist 2 entries to be awarded a grant to showcase their installation at Conscious Collective 2024. The final designs will also be showcased in the print edition of Architecture + Design magazine. Students are invited to send in attested their entries, attested by their institute on the basis of 2 categories listed below:

1. Built Spaces
Spaces conceived & built keeping in mind ecological compatibility. The category invites entries for net-zero buildings & built spaces that ensure reduced carbon footprint.

2. Materials
Materials plays an important role in the practice of architecture & design. This category will recognize efforts in experimenting with new conscious materials, exploring synergy between new & old - all the practices that contribute to create thought-through spaces.

In keeping with the theme of Conscious Collective all installations will be fabricated using waste materials from the Godrej Enterprises Group facilities.

Terms & Conditions:

  • Entries must be attested by an accreted institution. Only one entry will be accepted per institution.

  • Design production to be done with waste materials provided.
  • Deadline for submissions: August 15, 2024
  • Entries to be sent to godrejconnect@burdaluxury.com
  • The entries should have the following details:
    • Concept note, including the theme and the objective
    • Concept sketches/ drawings/ renders
    • Dimensions of the installation
    • Working model installations and pavilion concepts for the site-specific installation.
    • List of waste materials being used
  • Winning entries will receive:
    • Grant of up to 3 lakhs INR along with a certificate of participation to be awarded*
    • The winning entries will be showcased at Conscious Collective 2024 where students will be felicitated.
    • The installation process will be documented and amplified on the Architecture+Design & Conscious Collective (@godrejdesignlab) social media handles
    • The winners to be featured in the print edition of Architecture+Design
    • Conscious Collective to take care of travel and stay of 5 representatives per institute

*installation production cost must be covered within the grant of Rs 3 Lakhs awarded to the winning entries "
