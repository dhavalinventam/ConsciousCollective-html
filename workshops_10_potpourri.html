Make Your Own Organic Potpourri

By Scrapshala

12 Dec 2025 | 10:00 AM - 06:30 PM

Did you ever imagine crafting your own fragrant potpourri in just 15 minutes? At Conscious Collective, Scrapshala invites you into a hands-on experience where you blend organically sourced dried flowers and natural elements into a soothing keepsake that’s uniquely yours. It’s simple, creative, and something you can carry home, a gentle reminder of mindful living.

Scrapshala, a women-led conscious brand, is rooted in sustainable upcycling and the revival of Indian handicraft traditions. Every product and experience is designed to benefit all involved, artisans, communities, and consumers alike, turning raw materials and artisanal skill into meaningful livelihoods and unforgettable memories.
