GridMats Cool Green Wall reimagines urban infrastructure as a living cooling system. Vertical greenery softens city surfaces, while integrated sensors monitor temperature, humidity, and air quality in real time. A dynamic misting mechanism cools the surrounding microclimate, illustrating how modular green walls can offer relief in increasingly heat-stressed cities.

Together, these projects exemplify Climate Collective's commitment to fostering a community of thriving innovators-startups and creators designing scalable, restorative solutions that are deeply rooted in local contexts and driven by ecological consciousness.

Climate Collective is the largest private climate tech startup support organization in South Asia. It builds an integrated ecosystem for early stage climate tech startups by providing acceleration programs, access to capital, community support, and opportunities for market deployment. Its mission is to nurture and empower climate tech entrepreneurs across the Global South, helping them transform innovative ideas into scalable, climate positive solutions.
